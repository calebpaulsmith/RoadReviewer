This tool classifies the road, never the project — a "Federal aid" tag means the road is on the federal-aid system (Urban Minor Collector or greater per FHWA functional classification and the 2020 Adjusted Census Urban Boundaries); the inspector or applicant decides what that means for any given work order. Road-class lookup is wired for Michigan (MDOT), Indiana (INDOT) and Wisconsin (WisDOT); other states get the urban-boundary check only. Map tiles © Esri (World Street Map / World Imagery) — viewing the map, or generating a PDF report, fetches tiles for the relevant area from Esri.
